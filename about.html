Our Story

Built by Builders,
For Builders.

BŌRYKU started from a simple frustration: finding the right gear for your specific truck was a mess. Too many brands, too many forums, too many guesses. We built the platform we always wanted.

Mission

Control The Chaos.

Outfitting an overland rig should feel like building something you're proud of — not navigating a chaotic web of incompatible parts and scattered information.

BŌRYKU brings together a curated ecosystem of premium brands, certified installers, and an intuitive build configurator so you can focus on what matters: building your machine exactly the way you want it.

We don't auto-generate builds or push random recommendations. We give you the tools to make your own choices — with the right information to make smart ones.

Your Build. Your Choices.

No auto-selections. Every product is manually chosen by you. The platform provides information, compatibility, and pricing — you decide what goes on your rig.

Real Compatibility Data

Products are matched to your exact vehicle, year, and trim. No guessing. No "fits most trucks" ambiguity.

Premium Brands Only

We curate. Every brand on the platform is vetted for quality, reliability, and community reputation in the overland space.
